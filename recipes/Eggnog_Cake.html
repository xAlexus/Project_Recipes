How to make Eggnog Cake

  • 250g Powdered Sugar
  • 5 egg(s)
  • 2 pck vanilla Sugar
  • 250 ml Oil
  • 250 ml eggnog
  • 250 g flour
  • 1 pck backing Powder
  1. Beat eggs until foamy, slowly stir in powdered sugar and vanilla sugar
  2. Sift flour and baking powder, slowly fold into egg mixture
  3. Slowly stir in oil. Pour batter into a loaf pan or wreath pan
  4. Baking time: 45-50 min. at 160 degrees Celcius
