How to make Eggnog Cake

    250g Powdered Sugar
    5 egg(s)
    2 pck vanilla Sugar
    250 ml Oil
    250 ml eggnog
    250 g flour
    1 pck backing Powder
    Beat eggs until foamy, slowly stir in powdered sugar and vanilla sugar.
    Sift flour and baking powder, slowly fold into egg mixture.
    Slowly stir in oil. Pour batter into a loaf pan or wreath pan.
    Baking time: 45-50 min. at 160 degrees Celcius
