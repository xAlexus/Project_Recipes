How to make Wheat Bread

  • 500 g wheat flour
  • 350 ml water, lukewarm
  • 1 tbsp. olive oil
  • 2 tsp. salt
  • 1 tsp. sugar
  • 1 Â½ cup/s milk
  • 1 bag/s of dry yeast
  1. Put the flour and all the other ingredients in a bowl and mix with a wooden spoon until the dough no longer sticks to the bowl
  2. Cover the bowl with a cloth and let the dough rise for 30 minutes. Line a 500 g loaf pan with baking paper
  3. After 30 minutes, flour the work surface, the dough and your hands. Lift the dough from the bowl and knead again by hand for tension
  4. Put the dough in the mold and smooth it with a pastry brush with a little water
  5. Make a lengthwise cut and place in a cold oven. The baking time starts when the baking temperature is reached, about 40 min. at 170 degrees top/bottom heat
