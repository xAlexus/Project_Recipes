How to make American Pancakes

  • 2 cup/s of flour, sifted
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 4 tsp. baking powder
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 2 egg(s), beaten
  • 1 ½ cup/s milk
  • ¼ cup/n oil
  1. First mix the dry ingredients. Then mix the wet ingredients and pour over the top
  2. Mix quickly until there are no big lumps. Heat some oil in a pan and take 2 spoonfuls of batter per cake
  3. Fry on electric stove on level 2. When the bubbles pop, check to see if the bottom is brown. If so, flip
  4. When the pancakes are ready, each person makes a small tower, always pancake first, then syrup, etc., until you have stacked so 3 - 4 pancakes
